Edward Yov is a Moldovan artist. He was born in 1975 in the city of Balti in the north of Moldova.

{{'From an early age, he had two occupations: expression and freedom of transmission of his thoughts to the canvas and high competition sport - weightlifting.'|_}}

{{'Due to various traumas, he abandoned sport and gave all his time to art.'|_}}

The artist works with different styles (cubism, classic art, impressionism, airbrush), and with different types of material such as (oil, acrylic, charcoal, gypsum).

In just a word, Edward transports his ideas born in his sleep to the new creations presented on canvas.
